## Chapter 3: The Trust Revolution

### Why Blockchain Changes Everything

**The Old World (Trust Required):**
- Stay at hotel → Trust they won't overcharge
- Leave review → Trust it's from real guest
- Book online → Trust OTA with your data
- Pay with card → Trust payment processor

**The New World (Trust Verified):**
- Stay at hotel → Smart contract enforces terms
- Leave review → Blockchain proves you stayed
- Book direct → Your data, your control
- Pay with crypto → Instant, irreversible, low fee

### Removing the "Trust Tax"

Every intermediary exists because of trust deficit:
- Banks exist because people don't trust each other with money
- OTAs exist because travelers don't trust unknown hotels
- Credit cards exist because merchants don't trust buyers

**Blockchain removes the need for trusted intermediaries.**

When trust is built into the protocol:
- 15-30% OTA fees → 1% network fee
- 2-4% card fees → 0.1% crypto fee
- Days to settle → Seconds to settle
