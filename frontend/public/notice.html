Starting March 1st, all guests are required to use our new
Self-Service Guest Portal for check-in and check-out.

The Guest Portal tablet is located at the front desk.

How to Use the Guest Portal

  • 1 First-Time Guests: Tap "Register" and enter your Employee Number & Name
  • 2 Check In: Tap "Check In", verify your Employee Number, enter room & time, sign
  • 3 Check Out: Tap "Check Out", enter your Room Number and time

Printed instructions are available at the kiosk.

For assistance, please contact front desk staff.
