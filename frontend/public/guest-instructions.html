1 REGISTER (First-Time Guests Only)
  1. Tap the GOLD "Register First Time User" button
  2. Enter your Employee Number
  3. Enter your Full Name
  4. Tap "Complete Registration"
Note: You only need to register once. After registering, you can check in anytime using your employee number.
2 CHECK IN
  1. Tap the GREEN "Check In" button
  2. Enter your Employee Number
  3. Tap "Verify" to confirm your identity
  4. Enter your assigned Room Number
  5. Select your Check-In Date using the calendar
  6. Enter your Check-In Time
  7. Sign your name in the Signature Box using your finger or stylus
  8. Tap "Complete Check-In"
TIME FORMAT: Use 24-hour military time
Examples: 14:00 = 2:00 PM | 08:00 = 8:00 AM | 22:30 = 10:30 PM
Important: Your signature will be used for both check-in and check-out records.
3 CHECK OUT
  1. Tap the RED "Check Out" button
  2. Enter your Room Number
  3. Select your Check-Out Date using the calendar
  4. Enter your Check-Out Time
  5. Tap "Complete Check-Out"
TIME FORMAT: Use 24-hour military time
Examples: 11:00 = 11:00 AM | 15:30 = 3:30 PM | 18:00 = 6:00 PM
Note: No signature is needed at check-out. Your check-in signature is automatically used.
4 VIEW SIGN-IN SHEET (Optional)
  1. Tap "View Sign-In Sheet" button
  2. Enter your Company Name or Employee Number
  3. Tap "View Sign-In Sheet" to access records
QUICK 24-HOUR TIME REFERENCE
12:00 AM = 00:00 12:00 PM = 12:00
6:00 AM = 06:00 6:00 PM = 18:00
8:00 AM = 08:00 8:00 PM = 20:00
10:00 AM = 10:00 10:00 PM = 22:00
