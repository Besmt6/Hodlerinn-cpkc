1. REGISTER (First-Time Only)
  1. Tap GOLD "Register" button
  2. Enter your Employee Number
  3. Enter your Full Name
  4. Tap "Complete Registration"
Note: Register once, then check in anytime with your employee number.
2. CHECK IN
  1. Tap GREEN "Check In" button
  2. Enter Employee Number → Tap "Verify"
  3. Enter your Room Number
  4. Select Check-In Date (calendar)
  5. Enter Check-In Time (24hr format)
  6. Sign in the Signature Box
  7. Tap "Complete Check-In"
Important: Your signature is used for both check-in and check-out.
3. CHECK OUT
  1. Tap RED "Check Out" button
  2. Enter your Room Number
  3. Select Check-Out Date (calendar)
  4. Enter Check-Out Time (24hr format)
  5. Tap "Complete Check-Out"
Note: No signature needed - your check-in signature is used automatically.
4. VIEW SIGN-IN SHEET (Optional)
  1. Tap "View Sign-In Sheet"
  2. Enter Company Name or Employee Number
  3. Tap "View Sign-In Sheet"
