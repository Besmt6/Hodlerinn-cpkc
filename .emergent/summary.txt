Build a two-part application, Hodler Inn, with a guest portal and an admin backend.

**Guest Portal Requirements:**
1.  A unified check-in flow for new and returning guests.
2.  Guests can check in even if not on the pre-approved employee list; they will be marked as pending verification.
3.  Guests check out by entering their room number.
4.  The portal provides voice guidance and visual feedback.
5.  A How to Use section with a video tutorial.

**Admin Backend Requirements:**
1.  A dashboard for guest activity reporting.
2.  Billing and reporting features (Excel, PNG, PDF), with record editing.
3.  Full CRUD for employee management, including bulk import.
4.  An AI agent to scrape historical data from a third-party railroad portal.
5.  An AI daily sync agent to verify stays against the railroad portal.
6.  Telegram notifications for check-ins.
7.  A bulk verification system for pending guests.
8.  Employee names in the admin list should be automatically updated to match the format on the railroad portal.
9.  An AI Phone Agent to automatically handle wake-up calls.
10. Automatic daily data backups (to email or cloud storage).
11. Automatic sold out email notifications to the railroad company.
12. Room management features, including blocking rooms for non-railroad guests and tracking cleaning status.
13. A Guarantee Report to track unused guaranteed rooms against the railroad contract.
